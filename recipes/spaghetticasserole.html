Spaghetti Casserole

Description

This easy spaghetti casserole dish with ground beef is deliciously rich, hearty, and cheesy.

This crowd-pleasing spaghetti casserole recipe will satisfy all your comfort food cravings.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this homemade spaghetti casserole recipe:

  • Spaghetti
  • Cream cheese and sour cream
  • Cheeses
  • Oil
  • Sirloin
  • Onion
  • Seasonings
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. Cook th beef until the meat is cooked through, then drain the grease.
  3. Stir the sauce into the beef mixture, then add the spaghetti and stir until coated in sauce.
  4. Assemble the spaghetti casserole according to the detailed instructions.
  5. Bake, covered with foil, for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove the foil, sprinkle with Parmesan, and broil until the cheese is golden brown.
